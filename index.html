SAI KUNG 30KM RUN

Sai Kung 30km Run is an annual trail running event organised by the Hong Kong Hiking Group on Sai Kung Peninsula, the easternmost part of Hong Kong. With elevation gain of more than 1,200m, participants get to push their limits as their eyes feast on the landscape of undulating hills and long coastline, untouched by the urbanization of Hong Kong.

The event starts and ends on Pak Tam Chung. Come and join us for the Sai Kung 30km Run on Dec 1, 2019.
